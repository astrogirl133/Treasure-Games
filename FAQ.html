FAQ

If you have any questions or want to know what we have in stock, see our contact information.

Q: What are your operating hours?

A: We are open Sunday though Saturday from 9:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Q: My game won't start! What should I do?

A: If the game is a cartrage, clean the connectors with a dry cotton swab. Do not blow into the cartrage. If it's a disc, make sure it's not scratched or dirty. If the disc is dirty, clean it with a soft cloth, dampened with water. Do not use a cleaning agent.

Q: What should I do if I want to make a return?

A: Make sure you have your receipt with you, and make sure the game is still in working condition. Games that are damaged beyond playability may not be accepted. If the game was defective at the time of purchase, you can either get a refund or have the game swapped for a working version free of chrage.
