About Treasure Games

Treasure Games was founded on July 2, 2007 by a group of friends. The group had decided to make a business of selling vintage video games for the best prices they could find when they themselves had to pay the outrageous prices for old games. Their mission become to provide the best experience possible at their store while also still providing the best prices.

As they went along with the process of starting a business, they ran into various obstacles as many new business owners do. They ran into obstacles such as finding their first building, finding and buying inventory, and getting customers to come to their store. They perserved through it all and now run two stores in different cities while also running an online website where customers can buy games. They continue to live their dream as they help customers to find their treasure every day.
